Khachapuri Recipe

Home

Description

Khachapuri is a traditional Georgian dish consisting of cheese-filled bread, often served with a side of sour cream.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (7g) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 cup grated cheese (Gruyère or mozzarella)
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • Sour cream for serving (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, yeast, and salt in a large bowl. Add warm water and stir until a dough forms.
  2. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes until smooth.
  3. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour or until doubled.
  4. Punch down the dough and roll it into a large ball. Roll out into a circle on a floured surface.
  5. Place grated cheese in the center of the rolled-out dough and fold edges over the cheese to form a pocket.
  6. Cut into wedges and place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Brush with melted butter.
  7. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Serve hot with sour cream if desired!
