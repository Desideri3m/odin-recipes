Million Dollar Soup Recipe

Home

Description

Million Dollar Soup is a rich and creamy soup made with a blend of vegetables, herbs, and a touch of luxury.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme
  • 1 teaspoon fresh rosemary
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, celery, potatoes)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions and saut√© until translucent.
  2. Add garlic, thyme, and rosemary. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Pour in vegetable broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Stir in heavy cream. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving. Enjoy!
