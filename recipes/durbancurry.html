Durban Curry Recipe

Description

Durban curry is a flavorful South African dish made with a rich, aromatic curry sauce and tender meat or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons Durban curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 500g chicken thighs or vegetables of choice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions and saut√© until translucent.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, cooking for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in Durban curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and coriander. Cook for 2 minutes to toast the spices.
  4. Add diced tomatoes and coconut milk, stirring to combine.
  5. Add chicken or vegetables, ensuring they are submerged in the sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through or vegetables are tender.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. Enjoy with rice or bread!
